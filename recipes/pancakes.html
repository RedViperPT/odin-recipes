Pancakes

Description

An amazing recipe for delicious fluffy pancakes

Read below for a list of ingredients and instruction

Ingredients

  • Flour 300g
  • Sugar 150g
  • Salt 2 tea spoons
  • Eggs 4 unit
  • Milk 400ml
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Mix the ingredientes in a bowl until you get a smooth consistency.
  2. Heat a pan over medium heat, add butter and let it melt. Add the pancake batter and spread it around the pan.
  3. When the edges are dry and the bubbles start popping flip the pancake, use a spatula if needed, and let it cook until browned.
