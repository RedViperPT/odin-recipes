Description

One of the most iconic Portuguese soups, either as an entrée or a full meal

Ingredients

  • Water 1,2 L
  • Potatoes 600 g
  • Onions 120g
  • Kale 200 g
  • Garlic 4 units
  • Olive Oil 50 ml
  • Chorizo 60g

Steps

  1. Peel the onion and garlic, chop them roughly and sauté them in a pan with two tablespoons of olive oil and the chorizo.
  2. Meanwhile, peel the potatoes and cut them into cubes.
  3. When the onion is soft and translucent, remove the chorizo and add the potatoes. Season with salt, cover, and let cook over very low heat for about 10 minutes. Pour in the boiling water and let it cook until the potato is tender.
  4. Meanwhile, place the kale in a mesh strainer and rinse it under running water.
  5. Blend the soup with an immersion blender until the potato is completely broken down. Return to the heat and when it returns to a boil, add the kale and let it cook for about 8 minutes with the pan uncovered.
  6. Drizzle the soup with the remaining olive oil and serve with the chorizo sliced.
